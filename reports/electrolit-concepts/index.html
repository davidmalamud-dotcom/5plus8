10 concept routes designed to make Electrolit powders impossible to ignore.

Creative Brief

Create performance creative that stops the scroll, lands the science, and still feels ownable to Electrolit.

  • Built for Meta-first paid social environments
  • Strong enough to work across static, motion, and short-form video
  • Distinct from generic sports hydration and generic wellness creative

Executive Read

The strongest ideas all do the same thing in different ways.

The most compelling routes do more than create attention. They translate Electrolit’s product truth into a creative system, either by making the science more vivid, making the hydration benefit more immediate, or turning portability into something memorable.

The opportunity is to create a set of territories that can produce distinctive high-performing assets without becoming repetitive or generic.

Evaluation Framework

Every route was measured against the same five standards.

01

Scroll-Stopping Power

Can the idea win attention quickly in-feed without needing extra setup?

02

Science Credibility

Does it give the 5-ion formula and hydration story a real place to live?

03

Brand Fit

Does it feel like Electrolit rather than borrowed category styling?

04

Audience Breadth

Can the route flex across broad use moments and still feel relevant?

05

Scalability

Can it create multiple executions across formats without losing energy?

Electrolit Advantage

What the creative should keep leveraging

Electrolit can uniquely combine scientific credibility with bold flavor energy.

  • The 5-ion formula, especially magnesium and calcium, gives the work real scientific footing.
  • Flavor cues create room for vibrant art direction that still feels product-connected.
  • Pharmaceutical heritage gives the brand permission to speak with more authority than most competitors.

Concept 01

Keep It in Your Back Pocket

Portability becomes both the product story and the creative hook.

This concept turns the powder format into an ownable idea: hydration you can carry, and knowledge you can keep close. It gives Electrolit a simple, memorable platform that can flex from product utility into education.

  • An ownable phrase rooted in a real product truth
  • Strong enough for static, motion, carousel, and creator formats
  • Practical and informative without feeling overly instructional

Concept 02

The Science of Hydration

Science becomes a visual world, not just a claim.

Instead of talking about the formula in a literal way, this route builds an art direction around it: structure, pattern, molecule-inspired compositions, and controlled energy. The result feels elevated, legible, and distinctly Electrolit.

  • The clearest home for the 5-ion story
  • Separates Electrolit from both wellness-soft and sports-loud creative
  • Creates a complete design system rather than a single campaign line

Concept 03

Return to Form

Hydration is framed as restoration, vitality, and visible renewal.

This route translates the benefit into a more emotional image system: depleted becomes restored, dull becomes vivid, out-of-form comes back to life. It gives Electrolit a more expressive lane without losing the hydration message.

  • Strong visual contrast between depletion and recovery
  • Elegant enough for still imagery and dramatic enough for motion
  • Emotionally resonant while staying benefit-led

Concept 06

Before and After

The hydration benefit is captured in one fast, legible visual shift.

This concept turns the product effect into instant contrast: flat to energized, muted to vivid, depleted to switched on. It is built for placements where the idea has to register immediately.

  • Fast to understand in-feed
  • High-contrast by design, which supports paid social performance
  • Flexible across different moments, moods, and use cases

Concept 04

For Every Moment

Hydration adapts to the occasion, the need state, and the person.

This concept turns Electrolit into a flexible lifestyle system. A modular character changes outfit, environment, and activity to show how the product can move across different moments while still feeling cohesive.

  • Playful and immediately legible
  • Easy to extend across flavors, audiences, and scenarios
  • Creates a broad platform for high-volume social execution

Concept 05

Proven Over Time

The brand story becomes a proof point, not just a backdrop.

This route frames Electrolit as a product built on science and sustained by credibility. By moving through the brand’s history and scientific foundation, it positions the product as established, trustworthy, and built to last.

  • Strongest route for heritage and credibility storytelling
  • Useful when the message needs more authority than hype
  • Well suited to timeline, stop-motion, and documentary-style execution

Concept 07

Keeps You on Beat

Hydration is expressed as rhythm, balance, and internal alignment.

Inspired by cymatics and vibration, this concept visualizes Electrolit as a force that brings the body back into sync. It gives the science story a more artistic and sensory expression.

  • Distinctive visual language with strong motion potential
  • Connects science to feeling rather than just information
  • Well suited to music-led, movement-led, and abstract executions

Concept 08

Hydration in Motion

One body, many moments, with hydration shown through continuous movement.

This route uses repeated motion within a single frame to show the product at work. Instead of describing hydration, it visualizes its effect through flow, energy, and uninterrupted movement.

  • Movement itself becomes the proof point
  • Useful for sports, lifestyle, and hybrid executions
  • Strong visual rhythm across stills, GIFs, and short-form video

Concept 09

Science Meets Hydration

Lab cues move directly into active lifestyle imagery.

This concept closes the gap between scientific credibility and everyday performance. By placing beakers and lab language inside real activity moments, it makes the science visible without losing energy or pace.

  • Makes the scientific story instantly recognizable
  • Creates bold contrast inside familiar sports and lifestyle scenes
  • Balances authority with visual punch

Concept 10

Dr. Frida

A recurring personality gives the science story charm, confidence, and edge.

This route introduces a character who can deliver hydration facts with attitude and social-native presence. It gives Electrolit a distinctive voice and a repeatable format for education-led content.

  • Character-led storytelling with strong creator energy
  • Easy to serialize across social content
  • Turns education into something more memorable and entertaining

Next Steps

Select three directions to expand into execution.

The next phase can focus on building out the three concepts that feel most aligned with the brand, the audience, and the kind of story Electrolit wants to tell.

From there, each selected direction can be developed into a fuller creative system across static, motion, and short-form video, with the next stage focused on how each concept behaves in execution rather than how it reads in summary.
